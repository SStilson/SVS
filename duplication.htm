DVD, Blu-ray and CD Duplication

We specialize in small duplication runs of 500 or less. Proud parents can make disc copies for family members. Companies can get their CD and DVD duplication order turned quickly enough to meet almost any deadline. We use only high quality CDs and DVDs, utilizing the latest in technology to ensure your satisfaction. We never use paper labels, but instead imprint directly onto the DVDs/CDs. Custom label designs and case options are available as well. We look forward to handling your next CD and DVD duplication project.

Some Advantages of our Duplication Service...

At Stilson Video Services, we don't penalize our customers with a minimum order charge. Need to stop by to have one disc copied? No problem.

Do you need help with label or packaging design? Our skilled graphic designer can help in the design of your black and white or full-color label. Our professional quality ink jet printer will then print your design directly on to the discs with stunning results.

When it comes to choosing cases or custom packaging, we offer a wide variety of options, including:

  • Amaray Style Cases
  • Jewel Cases
  • Slimline Jewel Cases
  • Plastic Flexible Cases
  • Paper Sleeves
  • Have your own case? No problem, we’ll just deliver the discs on spindles.

Please call us today to discuss your next CD or DVD duplication project.

Where’s the pricing? We’ve found that a one-price-fits-all pricelist approach doesn’t fit this kind of service. Every project is vastly different and must be priced accordingly. This allows us to give our best price to each customer.
