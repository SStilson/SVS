Audio Cassette Tape to CD Transfer

Our professional quality audio transfer equipment ensures the highest possible quality from your audio cassette tapes. If needed, we can also duplicate audio cassettes as well. Believe it or not, there is still an occasional need for that service and who are we to question ‘why?’.

Reel-to-Reel Audio to CD Transfer

Our professionally maintained reel-to-reel audio equipment allows us to get the best recording from your audio reels. If needed, we can edit the audio as well to adjust speed and certain kinds of distortion.

Record Albums Converted to CD

There are a multitude of albums that simply don't exist for purchase on CD or digital download. In these cases, we have high quality transfer equipment to play your 33 1/3, 45 or even 78 albums and record them to CD. Not only does this preserve the music quality, but makes listening to them far more convenient!

Where’s the pricing? We’ve found that a one-price-fits-all pricelist approach doesn’t fit this kind of service. Every project is vastly different and must be priced accordingly. This allows us to give our best price to each customer.
