Event Presentations

We are often told that our photomontages were the highlight of the event and are watched multiple times as encores! In fact, we can set-up your video so it plays in a continuous loop.

Not only is the value of this type of video experienced initially, but it also serves as an archived memory that can be enjoyed year after year. Many times our customers request copies to share with family members or to give as gifts for the holidays. Photo Video Montages are perfect for:

  • Weddings
  • Graduations
  • Birthdays
  • Anniversaries
  • Memorials
  • Company Events
  • Bar/Bat Mitzvahs
  • General Photo Archiving
  • Sports Banquets
  • Reunions
  • Family Histories, etc.
Anything that can be photographed or recorded can be used in a photo video montage, including:
  • All consumer formats of camcorder video tape
  • 8 and 16mm home movie film
  • Photos of all sizes (prints or digital files)
  • 35mm slides
  • Newspaper clippings, certificates, etc.
Once you've gathered your materials for the video it's time to sort them and get them into the proper order. Consider breaking the video up in to several groups of subject matter. Titling each group will help the viewer follow along. Also, consider what music selections you would like to use for each section. If you get stuck along the way, we can certainly offer ideas based on our experience. At this point, it's time to bring your materials in to the studio so we can discuss all the finer details of the project.

After we've created the video, we give you the opportunity to have a private viewing to make sure everything is perfect. At this point, we can still make a change if needed which gives our customers peace of mind. Usually, our customers are involved in planning other phases of the upcoming event so we are mindful of making this process simple and painless–and fun! At Stilson Video Services, all of our photo video montages are custom productions because our customers’ ideas and expectations are all different. A cookie cutter approach would never work.

Please give us a call today to discuss a highlight of your next event!

Where’s the pricing? We’ve found that a one-price-fits-all pricelist approach doesn’t fit this kind of service. Every project is vastly different and must be priced accordingly. This allows us to give our best price to each customer.
