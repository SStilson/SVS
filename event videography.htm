Event Videography

Let Us Preserve the Memory of Your Next Special Event

We offer a complete line of video production services ranging from quality video coverage of an event to multi-camera high definition shoots with custom audio and lighting setups. There's no better way to preserve the memory of a special event than having it professionally videotaped. Whether it's a wedding, Bar/Bat Mitzvah, birthday party, anniversary, reunion or corporate event, we have the experience to deliver a first-class memory of your event to be enjoyed again and again.

We videotape school plays and sporting events, concerts, dance recitals, focus groups, business seminars and training classes. We can even videotape a family member to produce a Life History Video, where we ultimately produce a documentary style video of that person's life. If needed, we can provide you with edited DVDs of your event in any quantity, including a variety of design and packaging options. If you would like your video to play on a website, we can provide it in that format as well.

If you want your event professionally videotaped, let us know as soon as possible so we can "lock" it in on the schedule. Give us a call today to discuss your video needs as you plan your next special event.

High School and College Sports

We have increasingly been asked to cover various sports teams in our area throughout their seasons. There are a few key advantages to hiring us to record your games for you instead of relying on parents or students:

  • Our videographers are professionally trained and most have years of experience covering sports.
  • We provide our videographers with the proper equipment to get the job done right.
  • In most cases, the coaches receive the game film immediately following the game so they can upload straight to Hudl or a similar online site.
  • The quality of a professionally shot video is a key advantage for those athletes who will showcase their skills to college coaches or professional organizations.

If you are considering outsourcing for game film this season, contact us today as scheduling becomes more difficult as time goes on.

Where’s the pricing? We’ve found that a one-price-fits-all pricelist approach doesn’t fit this kind of service. Every project is vastly different and must be priced accordingly. This allows us to give our best price to each customer.
